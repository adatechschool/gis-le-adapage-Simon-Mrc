Valley Uprising is a must-watch if you’re even a little curious about climbing, counterculture, or how a small group of dirtbag rebels reshaped an entire sport. Set in Yosemite, the film blends stunning footage with sharp humor.

You should watch Silence because it’s an intense, honest look at the edge of human performance and obsession. The film follows Adam Ondra as he attempts the world’s first 9c climbing route.

Free Solo is a breathtaking and deeply human story about confronting fear at its purest. The film follows Alex Honnold’s attempt to climb El Capitan without ropes.

The Dawn Wall because it’s one of the most inspiring stories ever told in climbing—and in perseverance. The film follows Tommy Caldwell and Kevin Jorgeson as they attempt an almost impossible ascent of El Capitan
